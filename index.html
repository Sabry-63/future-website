About Us

Future Entertainment Works Co. was established in 1998 to serve the fast growing sector of home entertainment systems in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

In 2010, the management decided to obtain the ISO certification (ISO 9001:2008), in order to protect the company’s significant growth and to further enhance the quality of work and customer perception.
