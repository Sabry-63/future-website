IoT

Internet of Things (IoT) is the most profound change in personal and enterprise IT since the creation of the Worldwide Web more than 20 years ago. Researchers estimate that there will be tens of billions devices (i.e. computers, smartphones, tablets, sensors… etc.) connected to the Internet by 2020. Future entertainment works co has adopted the IOT from its early stages, moving our automation solutions into the next generation, providing our clients with extensive monitoring and alerting services in cases of fire, burglary, flood or gas leakages, as well as custom child and senior care solutions. Moreover, advantages of IoT extends into energy saving by monitoring consumption, allowing remote control and automation of lights, temperature and all other powered devices
